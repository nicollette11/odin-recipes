Sweet Potato Curry

Description:

Be prepared to meet a soup that is the perfect mix of hearty and creamy with sweet potato, chickpeas, coconut milk, and red curry paste. This is a healthy meal for the whole family and perfect as a winter soup. It can be served alone or on top of a bowl jasmine rice. If you are looking to add some extra protein, sautee some chicken or tofu and add into as well!

Store in an airtight container in fridge for up to 5 days
