Sweet Potato Curry

Description:

Be prepared to meet a soup that is the perfect mix of hearty and creamy with sweet potato, chickpeas, coconut milk, and red curry paste. This is a healthy meal for the whole family and perfect as a winter soup. It can be served alone or on top of a bowl jasmine rice. If you are looking to add some extra protein, sautee some chicken or tofu and add into as well!

Store in an airtight container in fridge for up to 5 days

Ingredients:

Serving Size: 8 people

  • 1 tbsp vegetable or coconut oil
  • 1 tbsp peeled and minced fresh ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp Thai red curry paste
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes (about 1lb total), peeled and cut into .5 inch cubes
  • 1 15oz can chickpeas, drained, rinsed
  • 1 14oz can full-fat coconut milk
  • .5 cup water
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • .25 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 5oz bag baby spinach (about 5 packed cups)
  • Juice from 1 medium lime (about 2 tbsps)
  • Cooked jasmine rice, for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat oild in large, deep skillet over medium heat until simmering
  2. Add the ginger and garlic and cook, stirring, until fragrant, about 1 minute. Stir in the curry past and saute for another minute
  3. Add the sweet potatoes, chickpeas, coconut milk, water, salt, and pepper and stir to combine. Bring mixture to a simmer, cover, and cook until the sweet potatoes are tender and cooked through, about 20 minutes.
  4. Stir in the spinach and cook until just wilted, 1 to 2 minutes. Remove from the heat and stir in the lime juice. Serve over rice if desired.
