Ingredients:

Serving size: 8 people

  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 teaspoon fresly grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • .5 red onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • .5 teaspoon kosher salt
  • .25 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tables spoons sugar, brown or coconut sugar
  • 1 table spoon light soy sauce, or liquid aminos
  • 1.5 cups red lentils
  • 4.5 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 13.5 oz can coconut milk
  • juice of 1 lime
  • .5 tablespoon sriracha, optional for heat
  • Top with lime and chopped purple cabbage, optional

Steps:

  1. In a pan over medium high heat, heat the sesame oil.
  2. Add in the minced ginger and curry paste, stirring into the oil for about 1 min or until fragrant
  3. Add in chopped onion and minced garlic, cook until onion is slightly translucent, about 5 minutes
  4. Add in the kosher salt, black pepper, sugar, and soy sauce and stir everything together. Add in the lentils and stir again.
  5. Add in coconut milk and veggie broth. Bring the soup to a boil and lower to simmer for about 20 to 30 minutes. Stir soup occasionally. You can let it simmer even more if desired
  6. Squeeze the lime over, stir, and then remove from heat. Taste and add more salt if needed.
  7. Add in any of the optional add ins/toppings. Stir in the Siracha or other hot sauce if using.
