Kid/Baby Friendly Chili

Description:

A mild chili that is delicious for your baby and the parents. It is packed with vegetables that are cook down into the meat sauce. It can be enjoyed as is or with chips and cheese. Add in your favorite hot sauce for extra spice.

Store in an airtight container in fridge for up to 3 days, or in freezer for up to 3 months

Ingredients:

Serving size: 6 people

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 white onion, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stick, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1lb lean ground beef
  • 3 tsp ground cumin
  • 3 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cups passata
  • 1 cup beef stock/broth
  • 1 medium zucchini, grated
  • 1 eggplant finely chopped
  • 1 14oz can kidney beans

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil over medium heat in large sauce pan.
  2. Add onion and celery. Saute for a few minutes until onions start to turn translucent.
  3. Add garlic and bell pepper, stir and allow it to cook for a few more minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Turn the heat up and add ground beef. Break the meat up in the pan and keep stirring until brown.
  5. Add the spices and stir for another minutes
  6. Add the tomato paste, passate, beef stock, zucchini, eggplant and kidney beans. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to a simmer and cover.
  7. Cook for at least one hour. Check occasionally to make sure sauce is not drying out or catching on bottom of the pan. If it is add a little more stock/broth.
  8. Season to taste.
