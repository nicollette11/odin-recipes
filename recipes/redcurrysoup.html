Vegan Coconut Curry Lentil Soup

Description:

This vegan coconut curry lentil soup is a savory dish that is perfect for the entire family. It is creamy, rich, and full of flavor. It is an excellent recipe to incorporate lentils into your diet. It can enjoyed on a bed of rice with chopped purple cabbage as a garnish and siracha hot sauce if you are looking to add some spice in your life.

Store in an airtight container in fridge for up to 5 days, or in freezer for up to 3 months
